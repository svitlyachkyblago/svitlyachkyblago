About Us

“Svitlyachky Blago” is about light. The light we want to give you and teach you to give to others, to notice it in the little things.

Our journey began in 2015 with a big idea and a small book of the same name “Svitlyachky” by Sira Arutyunyan-Bozoyan. It started the organization with the first charity auction on January 5, 2016.

In 2018, “Svitlyachky” was on the verge of closure because the founder fell seriously ill... But the organization not only survived, but was officially registered.

From 2018 to early 2022, we continued to implement projects...

But the morning of February 24, 2022 changed everything. The focus shifted to helping civilians and the Armed Forces.
