About Us

"Svitlyachky Blago" (Fireflies of Good) is about light. The light we want to give you and teach you to share with others, noticing it in the little things.

Our journey began in 2015 with a great idea and a small book of the same name. Over time, the organization grew from small charity auctions to large-scale social projects.

With the onset of the full-scale invasion, our focus immediately shifted. We opened points for receiving and issuing humanitarian aid, and organized the delivery of essential medicines and aid for the military.
